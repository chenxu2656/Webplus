In order to further promote the education and management of international students, and encourage them to cultivate their morality and study diligently, the project on the scholarship evaluation of international students at Anhui Medical University will be set up according to the related documents of university, combined with the characteristics of the international students. I Evaluated Object: The full-time international students at Anhui Medical University II Evaluation Criteria: Study hard and the annual comprehensive evaluation is above average; Abide by the laws and rules of China and the regulations of the university; Respect teachers, unite classmates and behave well; Value Chinese customs and maintain the friendship between the people from all over the world; Participate actively in activities organized by the school and the university.
